Privacy Policy — Indiamart Lead Notifier

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Indiamart Lead Notifier ("the extension") is a Chrome extension that monitors a seller's IndiaMART account for new buy leads matching the seller's saved filters, automatically claims (purchases) matching leads on IndiaMART, and sends the seller a phone notification with the buyer's contact details. This policy explains what data the extension collects, how it is used, and who it is shared with.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Your Google account email address, used to sign in and identify your account.
  • The phone number(s) you register to receive lead alerts.
  • Your lead filter settings (minimum price, minimum quantity, maximum lead age, states, keyword filters).
  • Basic device information (device name/type) for each computer or phone you register, so we can enforce your device seat limit.

Information collected from IndiaMART on your behalf:

When the extension is running, it reads new buy-lead data from your own, already-logged-in IndiaMART seller session and — for leads that match your filters — automatically purchases them using your IndiaMART account balance. In the course of doing this, it collects:

  • The buyer's name and mobile number
  • The buyer's city and state
  • The product/lead title, requested quantity, and approximate order value

This data originates from your IndiaMART seller account, not from the buyer directly, and is collected solely to notify you about leads you have already chosen to purchase.

Authentication data:

  • A Google-issued identity token, used to verify your sign-in and link your registered devices to your account.

2. How We Use This Information

We use the information described above only to:

  • Detect and automatically purchase IndiaMART buy leads matching your filters
  • Deliver phone notifications about purchased leads
  • Verify your sign-in and enforce your subscription/device seat limits
  • Persist your settings between sessions

We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the above.

3. Where Data Is Stored

  • On your device: lead history and app settings are stored locally in your browser (IndexedDB / chrome.storage.local).
  • Firebase Realtime Database (Google Cloud): your account's leads, registered devices, and entitlement status are stored here so alerts can reach your phone in real time.

4. Third-Party Services

The extension shares data with the following service providers, solely to deliver the features described above:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and real-time data storage.
  • Expo Push Notification Service (exp.host) — delivers push notifications (including the buyer's name/city/state) to your registered phone(s).
  • IndiaMART (seller.indiamart.com) — the source of lead data and the destination of lead-purchase requests, made using your own authenticated session.

We do not sell, rent, or transfer this data to any other third party, and we do not use it to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Lead history and settings are retained until you clear them or uninstall the extension. To request deletion of data associated with your account (including Firebase-stored lead history and device registrations), contact us using the details below.

6. Security

Data in transit is sent over HTTPS. The extension never asks for or stores your IndiaMART password — it acts within your existing, already-authenticated browser session.

7. Children's Privacy

This extension is intended for business use by IndiaMART sellers and is not directed at children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
